Our Story

From a small consultancy to national leadership

Founded in 2003 by a team of environmental engineers in Pune, GM Enviro started with a simple belief — that compliance and competitiveness can co-exist. Today we serve 120+ industrial clients across 15 states, having delivered 450+ projects spanning EIA, ETP/STP, pollution control and ESG advisory.

Our growth was never about scale alone. It was about staying close enough to every project to ensure measurable environmental and business outcomes.

Our Vision

To be India's most trusted environmental engineering partner — building a future where every industry operates in harmony with the planet.

Our Mission

To deliver scientifically sound, economically viable, and ethically responsible environmental solutions that create lasting impact for our clients and communities.
